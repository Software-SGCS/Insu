Bush: Vietnam offers Iraq lessons

U.S. President George W. Bush said Friday the United States' unsuccessful war in Vietnam three decades ago offered lessons for the American-led struggle in Iraq. "We'll succeed unless we quit," Bush said shortly after arriving in Vietnam's one-time war capital Hanoi.
